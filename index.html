About

Hi there, I am a second-year PhD student supervised by Prof. Simon Lucey at Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), University of Adelaide. Prior to this, I completed my Master's degree by research in Applied Mathematics, supervised by Prof. Nir Sochen at Tel Aviv University, Israel. Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Tel Aviv University, Israel.

My research focus on representation learning and training deep networks efficiently and effectively. I am current exploring training residual-free transformers.
